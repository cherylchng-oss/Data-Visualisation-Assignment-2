Background

Speeding is one of the most common contributors to road trauma. Australian states collect infringement data annually, including camera-issued fines, police-issued fines, and other detection categories. Since 2008, improvements in data collection allow more meaningful trend and jurisdiction comparisons.

Some years show data variability due to policy changes or reporting adjustments.

Data scope

  • Jurisdictions (NSW, VIC, QLD, etc.)
  • Time span: 2008–2024
  • Measures: counts & rates per 10,000 licences
  • Detection: camera vs police vs unknown

Aim

Provide an intuitive public dashboard that helps students, policymakers, and the community understand speeding enforcement patterns across Australia — and use those insights to ask better questions about road safety.
