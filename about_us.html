About Us

As part of our Data Visualisation Project (COS30045) at Swinburne University of Technology, Sarawak Campus, we — Cheryl and Cindy — present “Speeding Dashboard”, an interactive visualisation built with D3.js, HTML, and CSS.

We explore Australia’s road safety enforcement data with a focus on speeding infringements (2008–2024). Our aim is to transform raw statistics into clearer visuals that show trends over time, jurisdiction differences, and the balance between camera-issued and police-issued fines.

We contributed equally across research, design, development, testing, and reporting, keeping the experience simple, accessible, and useful for students and the public.
